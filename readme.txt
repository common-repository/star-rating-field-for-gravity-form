== Description ==

<span>Star Rating Field For Gravity Form</span> is free plugin. Star Rating Fields are added to Gravity Form by this plugin. Select a Rating style from 12 available options and set a few more parameters for your Rating Field. Downloading the Gravity Form with the Star Rating Field is free here. Gravity Form Star Rating Field in use all fields and are really simple to use.

Rating Field For Gravity Form is also very common to find businesses asking people to rating gravity form their services or products. This is due to the fact that consumers are more likely to purchase a product if they read honest evaluations from others who have really tried it.

<h3>Features Star Rating Field For Gravity Form:</h3>

<ul>
<li>Easy use</li>
<li>max value of rating (optional, 5 by default)</li>
<li>Clear or Cancel Button</li>
<li>Mobile Friedly</li>
<li>Select different type of star on and off</li>
<li>Unlimited rating fields in one form</li>
<li>Default rating</li>
<li>Single star selection</li>
<li>Enable/ disable cancel button place</li>
<li>Rating Title, Notice</li>
<li>Make Rating Required</li>
</ul>
